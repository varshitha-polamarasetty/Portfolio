Guess the number game

## 🎮 Description This is a simple terminal-based number guessing game written in C. The computer randomly selects a number between **1 and 100**, and the player has to guess it. ### Rules: - You have **unlimited attempts**. - After each guess: - If your guess is **within ±10** of the actual number → **Hot** - If your guess is **more than 10 away** → **Cold** - The game ends when the correct number is guessed.

Achievements

1st Place - Young Indians Future 5.0

As part of the prestigious Future 5.0 initiative by Young Indians (Yi) under CII (Confederation of Indian Industry), we had the opportunity to ideate and present a solution for one of the most pressing issues of modern urban life — the rising urban temperatures and vanishing green spaces. Our team focused on the increasing threat of urban heat islands caused by concrete expansion, reduced vegetation, and poor city planning. We proposed a powerful, sustainable solution — the integration of Miyawaki forests in metro and semi-urban areas. The Miyawaki method, a Japanese afforestation technique, allows dense and diverse forests to grow 10x faster and become 30x denser than conventional plantations, making it an ideal strategy to cool down cities naturally, improve biodiversity, and enhance air quality. We pitched a community-driven model where students, local citizens, and NGOs collaborate with municipal bodies to adopt unused urban plots and transform them into mini-forests. The idea was appreciated for its feasibility, sustainability, and social impact, aligning perfectly with Yi’s vision of nurturing young minds to lead India's future responsibly.
